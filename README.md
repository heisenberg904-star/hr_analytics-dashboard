# **HR Analytics Dashboard: Employee Attrition Analysis and Insights**

### **Overview**
The **HR Analytics Dashboard** is an interactive data visualization tool designed to analyze employee attrition patterns within an organization. By leveraging key metrics such as age, gender, education, job roles, and salary, this dashboard helps HR teams identify critical areas of concern and implement targeted strategies to enhance employee retention and satisfaction.

---

## **Features**
- **Comprehensive Metrics:** Visualize key insights like total employee count, attrition rate, average age, salary, and tenure.
- **Demographic Analysis:** Breakdown of attrition by age, gender, education, and salary brackets.
- **Job Role Insights:** Understand attrition trends across various job roles to identify high-risk positions.
- **Interactive Visualizations:** Charts and graphs for clear representation of trends, enabling actionable decision-making.
- **Actionable Recommendations:** Insights to support retention strategies based on the data.

## **Insights and Recommendations**
### **Key Insights**
1. **High Attrition Among Young Employees:**  
   Employees aged 26–35 experience the highest turnover, indicating challenges in early-career engagement.
   
2. **Salary Disparity:**  
   The majority of attrition occurs in the lowest salary bracket (up to $5K), highlighting financial dissatisfaction.

3. **Role-Specific Turnover:**  
   Positions like Laboratory Technician and Sales Executive face significant attrition, requiring job-specific interventions.

4. **Educational Impact:**  
   Employees with Life Sciences and Medical backgrounds show higher attrition, potentially due to unmet career expectations.

### **Recommendations**
- **Improve Retention for Key Age Groups:**  
  Offer mentorship, career development opportunities, and flexible work policies for employees aged 26–35.
  
- **Salary Adjustments:**  
  Reassess pay structures for employees earning up to $5K to improve satisfaction.

- **Focus on High-Risk Roles:**  
  Provide additional training, incentives, and career growth paths for Laboratory Technicians and Sales Executives.

- **Education-Specific Programs:**  
  Tailor retention strategies for employees with Life Sciences and Medical degrees, such as industry-specific development plans.
